How to Bridge

Here are a few steps that will get started on Arbitrum.

  • Step 1: Sending Ethereum from the L1 to the L2 with the Bridge

    Arbitrum is a L2 and requires you to bridge some tokens before being able to purchase tokens. You can use Arbitrum Native Bridge or the Celer Bridge to move your tokens to Arbitrum.

  • Step 2: Change Network In Metamask

    It may take a few minutes (~15min with the Optimism Bridge, 5 minutes with Celer Bridge) for your tokens to arrive on Arbitrum. When your tokens arrive on Arbitrum, you will be able to see them by adding the ARB network to your metamask. For that, open your wallet and select networks - - Add a network - - then select Arbitrum.

  • Step 3: Trading your tokens for $JSX

    Once you switched networks and received your $ETH from the bridge, you can buy any token on Arbitrum. Just make sure to connect on the right network and search for a token by address or name.
