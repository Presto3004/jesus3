FAQs

What is JESUS.3 and its native token $JSX

$JSX is a ERC-20 token available on Optimism that you can currently buy on Uniswap.

How do I move ETH to Optimism ?

Optimism is a different chain and requires you to bridge some Ethereum before being able to purchase tokens. You can use the official Optimism Bridge or the Celer Bridge to move Ethereum from the Ethereum Mainnet to Optimism.

How do I buy $JSX ?

You can buy $JSX by clicking the "Buy $JSX" button from our website. Always make sure you're using the right contract communicated on our website and in our official channels.

Why JESUS.3 is on Optimum ?

Avax had Wonderland / With the nice dynamic on Optimism, it perfectly makes sens for us to land there.

Which kind of wallet do I need ?

Metamask, TrustWallet and other web3 wallets natively support Optimism.

Can I get my ETH back to Ethereum ?

Yes of course ! You can bridge back your ETH through the bridge with the same way that you brought them on Optimism. You can use the official Optimism Bridge but it will take approximately 8 days. Otherwise, you can use the Celer Bridge and it will take few minutes (CELER charge tiny extra fees).

How do I stake $JSX ?

Once you have bought $JSX, you just have to access to our Staking Dashboard from our website by clicking on "Stake Tokens". Please, make sure to verify you're on the correct URL before approving any transaction : the devil is always around !

What is $BREAD ?

$BREAD is the token you receive when you stack your $JSX. The quantity of $BREAD you have will increase with the stacking. When you want to unstake, it will automatically convert your $BREAD is $JSX. $BREAD is $JSX, $JSX is $BREAD.

How do I unstake $JSX ?

Same way that you have stacked it. One button - we are blessed !
